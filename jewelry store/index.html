Our story

We’re a close-knit team of creatives based in New York, USA who use diamonds and gold to share stories.
That’s why quality is our top priority and we present delicate, made-to-order jewelry, from our hands to yours.
In just over a decade, our passion for jewelry-making went from being a hobby to becoming a successful operation that makes high-quality jewelry experiences for thousands of people all over the world. POSH Fine Jewelry won the “Creative Concepts in Retailing Award” from the Retailers Association of Massachusetts’ in 2019. And in 2020, Inc. Magazine recognized us as being one of the fastest-growing private companies in America, with 731% revenue growth in just three years.
